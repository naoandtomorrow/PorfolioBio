About Naoling

Throughout my life I've always wanted to be a professional problem solver, I just didn't know what specific area of problem-solving I wanted to be in. At first, I wanted to be a criminal defense lawyer, I attended a legal studies magnet program throughout high school, I was a part of the debate club, chess club, and mock trial. After high school, I received the American Dream scholarship for being a first-generation immigrant. With that I decided to get my associate's degree in psychology, I loved learning about the human mind and why we behave the way we do, why we constructed social cues and was planning on using that as a base starter for my career as an attorney. But, three semesters before my graduation I got a job as a traveling data analyst and I loved it.

For the last two years, I have been moving from state to state working as a data analyst for a staffing company with government contracts. My first contract was in Los Angeles, California, my second one was in Chicago, Illinois and lastly, I was in San Francisco, California. Each contract taught me new abilities, and as I continued to learn, I ventured into the world of independent work. I discovered that my preferred way of working more efficiently was the atmosphere of my computer, a deadline, and me. I researched for weeks on data analyst courses, but I found something that called me more: Software Engineering courses. I ran into Thinkful's course and knew it was perfect. As a student at Thinkful, I've begun my journey to becoming a software developer and couldn't be more ecstatic. If you're looking to hire a new developer, please reach out! I'm very excited to learn from others and share my knowledge to build transformative software.
